The purpose of DormWorks is to connect college students with remote paid internship opportunities from around the globe. Whether it be with Silicon Valley startups or international corporations, students with the right skillsets should have the opportunity to work with the companies they want to, when they want to, regardless of location.

Why dorm.works?

Time

For almost any student the biggest problem they face is scheduling conflicts. Most students are attempting to balance rigorous course work with club activities, Greek life, or even athletics, all while maintaining interest in their own personal ventures. A remote internship allows students to work the way they really want to, on their schedule.

Location

With a large number of universities located in rural areas, it is difficult for students to stay close to campus and work for the companies that they want to. A remote internship would allow a student to work for any company regardless of location. Whether is be working for Silicon Valley startups or global corporations, remote internships can open all doors.

Future

According to Forbes, more than one in five Americans work from their own home, and that number will only continue to grow. A remote internship can prepare students for the type of work they will be doing throughout their career. Remote work is the future of work in America, and the best way to get prepared for later is getting involved now.
